What I've Done So Far!

BusMe



BusMe is an application that makes it easier to view and ride nearby bus stops!

Passengers are able to check-in to the bus on the website. The application will search all nearby bus stops and the passenger is then able to select a stop that they want to check in at, along with how many people will be riding in their group. Then, the bus driver will be alerted of the number of people waiting at that stop so that they know to stop there to pickup the passengers!

This program was created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Express.JS, Node.JS & MySQL

For more information, feel free to visit busme.app to try it out for yourself!

Let's Meet

Let's Meet is a simplied schedule planner for busy people!

Having trouble finding a time where everybody in your team is free for a meeting? With Let's Meet,you're able to easily pass around an online grid that allows each member to input their available time throughout the week!

This program was created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Express.JS, Node.JS, NunJucks.JS, WebSocket, & MySQL

PowerPoint Hub

PowerPoint Hub is designed for University of the Pacific students who are ambitious and would like to view lecture slides from a course in a different major!

Many students would like to take courses that they are interested in, but are unable to due to major requirements and limited time. With PowerPoint Hub, you are able to view specific courses within a major that you are interested in!
