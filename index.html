    BusMe



    BusMe is an application that makes it easier to view and ride nearby bus stops!

    Passengers are able to check-in to the bus on the website. The application will search all nearby bus stops and the passenger is then able to select a stop that they want to check in at, along with how many people will be riding in their group. Then, the bus driver will be alerted of the number of people waiting at that stop so that they know to stop there to pickup the passengers!
